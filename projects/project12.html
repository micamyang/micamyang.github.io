Unity-Based Game Development

10.04.2024 ~ 21.06.2024

Overview

“Maze Escape No.1” is a survival puzzle adventure PC game set against the backdrop of Egyptian mythology and history. Players take on the role of an archaeologist who must navigate through deep sections of a pyramid, overcoming various obstacles and traps to escape the maze. The project aimed to deliver an immersive player experience through detailed level design (e.g. secret chambers, the gods’ room, and Pharaoh’s curse) and an intuitive user interface. As the Project Manager (PM), I successfully led the overall planning, progress management, and coordination between team members.

My Role

Game planning and project management (PM) using the Unity engine

Skills

Presentation, project management and leadership, teamwork and communication, planning and documentation, understanding of technical project execution
